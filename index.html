I knew you were the one when I saw myself better with you than I am with myself. You are the color I want to paint myself with, the coffee I’d look for each morning, the song I’d play on repeat, the taste my lips long for, the beauty my eyes seek to forevermore gaze. You are everything to me. I am yours, always. My heart that burns only for you will never extinguish it shal forever remain an ember; scorching.

at night when you feel lost; to the darkness you succumb, let me be your light. If your victory against the fiends is not granted, let me wield the sword of aid to fend them off.
When you will walk through the garden of fear, let my words be your shield to feel brave. I’d breath death and bleed my lungs to feed the flowers I made for you. I am steadfastly devoted to attending to you, and will forevermore be.
Although I am far, I am one call away.

Sharp lies to wound your heart is not what I am made of, nor am I made of jigsaw to keep your mind puzzled. I will forever hold truth; my honesty shall always reign. You will see me in the garden where the rumors fade away; in the sanctuary of truth where sincerity never dies. Just like my love for you, I am genuine and sincere.

You are who I remember each time I embrace the comfort of my bed. You are who I think of when I look at the purple skies. Your smell is what I want to breath. Your touch is the sensation I seek. Your hand is what mine wants to always hold. And when I remember love, I remember you. My love for you, it’s grown intense, that if I were deaf, I’d still hear my heart screaming your name. It’s you, and you, and you, over and over and over again.

I will handle your feelings as gentle as a dandelion, this I promise. I will hold your emotions like how I would hold the moon. Shall you ask for space, I’d give you the milky way. In the midst of war of which you were the wicked, still, I’d fight with you. In the language of which only you speak, I am not in need for translation; I will forever comprehend your soul.

To you I pledge, to thrive the hardest, compete with the best, give my outmost performance, to become the man you prayed for. Throw me the skills needed to give you a life you desire, and I shall learn it. To my very breath, I will thrive to win your heart.
And when I have nothing left to give, I will sell my soul.

Every part of you is a piece of art, be it the ones that are broken, or the ones that are glorious. Every piece of you is worth to know. I am always driven by curiosity of how and why and what you are exactly. You are as interesting as life. Like a puzzle from the web that no one could ever crack, I find glee in knowing you. I will always be in the interest of you.

Let me be the soil, and you be the tree, like a garden, your dreams will bloom, nurtured by my love, dispelling bloom. Every step you take, I will be there to cheer, endlessly encouraging you to conquer fear. Together, let us build a world so bright, where both our ambitions take flight.

Be our love the canvass, with roses in my hands and the stars above us, I will paint our story with endless love. Romantic gestures that shall never die, big and small, this is a love story to enthrall. From every kiss on sunrise to every hug on moonlit walks, romance bloom in every thought. From the bottom of my heart, I will love you.

Like the sun with its lights never flickering, my respect for you remains timeless. You are an honor; in your side I shall stand. Your strength, your wisdom, I will forever admire, with support that’s unwavering, lifting you higher. I will love you the same way I loved my parents, you are a valuable treasure, a goddess if i will.

I will dance you in the rain, laugh with you in the sun, kiss you under the moon, ease with you under the stars. I promise a life with joyful spree, a life of which we can say it was worth it. From pillow forts to midnight escapades, together, we'll live in endless parades. I will lit a smile on your face, hug you on your tiring days, kiss you on your gloomy ones. If why you might ask, it's simply because you look always the prettiest when you smile.

I will be your pillow who knows every tear you’ve shed. I will be your bed to which you run to when tired. I will be your friend to whom you tell your secrets to. I will be your lover who will take care of you. I want to see your scars and love you even more. I want to be there when you don’t feel good at all. I will be there.

Oh! My profound love for you will never wear off. I will never get tired of loving you; it is pure joy. To love you is a blessing, to be loved by you is a miracle. Like a sunset in a long road, I will chase you. Even when the winter whispers, the summer becomes cruel, the leaves begin to fall, and the flowers bloom, my love will stand unyielding, in its everlasting seasons.
